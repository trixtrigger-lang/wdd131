Visual Design & Accessibility

Good visual design helps users understand content quickly and easily. Layout, spacing, alignment, and contrast all play an important role in accessibility and usability.

Accessibility is not optional. Color contrast, readable fonts, and clear structure ensure content can be used by everyone.
